Veg. Biryani

Description

Biryani is one of the most popular dishes in South Asia, as well as among the diaspora from the region. It has gained popularity in South India

Ingredients

  • Curd
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Biryani Masala
  • Salt
  • Tumeric
  • Red chilli powder

Steps

  1. Soak the basmati rice for 30-45 minutes before cooking it in a pressure cooker.
  2. Alternatively, you can also add almost cooked basmati rice and layer it on top gravy and simmer in pressure cooker for 10 minutes.
  3. Lastly, you can also marinate the veggies for 30 minutes in curd / yogurt with dry spices powder
