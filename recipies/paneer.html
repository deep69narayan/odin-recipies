Tawa Paneer

Description

The entire recipe is cooked on a tawa, a flat or concave-shaped round pan; an essential piece of utensil in any Indian kitchen.

Ingredients

  • Paneer Cubes
  • Tomato Masala
  • Capsicum
  • Spices
  • Green Chilli

Steps

  1. Add chopped tomatoes in a blender and make a puree
  2. Chop one large onion and capsicum
  3. Chop one green chilli
  4. Crush 1 inch ginger and 4 to 5 small to medium garlic cloves in a mortar-pestle to a paste.
  5. Melt 2 tablespoons butter on a tawa
  6. Add carom seeds and fry them for a few seconds.
  7. Add finely chopped onions
  8. Add the prepared tomato puree and mix well
  9. Season with salt as required and saute with stirring at intervals
  10. Saute till you see butter releasing from the sides and the masala has thickened a bit.
